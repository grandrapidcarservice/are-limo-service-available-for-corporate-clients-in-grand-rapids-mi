Q1: What is the difference between a limo and a regular taxi?

A taxi is a yellow car that anyone can use. A limo is a fancy car that you book ahead of time. Limos are cleaner, more comfortable, and the driver is more professional. Taxis are cheaper but not as nice.

Q2: Can I use a limo service for my company's party or event?

Yes, you can. Many limo services help with company parties, weddings, and big events. You can ask the company about special prices for group rides.

Q3: How much does it cost to use a limo service in Grand Rapids for one hour?

The price changes. It depends on the type of car and how busy the day is. Most companies charge between 75 to 150 dollars per hour. You should call and ask for a real price.

Q4: Do I need to give a tip to the limo driver?

Yes, it is nice to give a tip if you are happy with the service. A tip of 15 to 20 percent is normal. If the driver was very good, you can give more.

Q5: Can I book a limo service for the whole day?

Yes, many companies offer full-day service. This is good if you have many meetings or a long trip. You can ask the company about daily rates, which are often cheaper than paying per hour.
